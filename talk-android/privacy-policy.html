Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SoftBlinks Talk ("we," "our," or "the App"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. SoftBlinks Talk is a secure communication platform that provides messaging, voice calls, video calls, and file sharing capabilities.

Important: SoftBlinks Talk is a client application that connects to SoftBLinks servers. We do not operate servers or store your data on our infrastructure. All data is stored on the SoftBLinks server you connect to (either your organization's server or a server of your choice).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Server URL, username, and authentication credentials (stored securely on your device)
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, and status (stored on your SoftBLinks server)
  • Communications: Messages, photos, videos, and files you share (stored on your SoftBLinks server)
  • Call Data: Voice and video call metadata (participants, duration, timestamps)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns, crash reports
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics for debugging and optimization

2.3 Permissions Required

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: To capture photos/videos and enable video calling
  • Microphone: To record voice messages and enable voice/video calls
  • Storage (Photos/Videos): To access and share media files in conversations
  • Contacts: To help you find and connect with other users (optional)
  • Notifications: To receive incoming call and message notifications
  • Full-Screen Intent: To display incoming call screens even when device is locked
  • Network Access: To connect to your SoftBLinks server and enable communication

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To connect you to your SoftBLinks server and enable communication features
  • Authentication: To securely authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Communication: To facilitate messaging, voice calls, video calls, and file sharing
  • Notifications: To send push notifications for incoming calls and messages
  • Performance: To monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain service security
  • Improvements: To improve app features and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Server Data: All messages, files, and call history are stored on YOUR SoftBLinks server (not our servers)
  • Local Device Storage: Authentication tokens, cached data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We Do Not Store: We do not operate servers or maintain databases of user data

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for calls (using WebRTC)
  • Secure HTTPS connections to your SoftBLinks server
  • Encrypted local storage for authentication credentials
  • Regular security updates and patches

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5.1 Information Shared with Your SoftBLinks Server

All communication data (messages, files, calls) is transmitted to and stored on the SoftBLinks server you connect to. Please review your SoftBLinks server's privacy policy for information about how that server handles your data.

5.2 Third-Party Services

  • Push Notifications: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google Play version only) to deliver push notifications
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash reports may be collected to improve app stability

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect our legal rights.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can access your data through your SoftBLinks server web interface
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and data by contacting your SoftBLinks server administrator
  • Permissions: You can revoke app permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in app settings
  • Data Export: You can export your data from your SoftBLinks server

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Data is transferred to and stored on the SoftBLinks server you connect to, which may be located in any country. The location and data protection practices depend on your chosen SoftBLinks server provider.

9. Data Retention

  • Messages and Files: Retained according to your SoftBLinks server's retention policies
  • Local Data: Cached data is cleared when you log out or uninstall the app
  • Call History: Stored on your SoftBLinks server according to server settings

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
